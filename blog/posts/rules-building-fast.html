My rules for building projects fast

When I’m stuck, I don’t need “more motivation”. I need constraints.

My rules

  • 1 page first: one screen, one purpose.
  • Ship ugly: then polish.
  • One feature per session: no multitasking.
  • Write it down: if it’s not written, it’s not real.

That’s it. Not sexy, but it works.
